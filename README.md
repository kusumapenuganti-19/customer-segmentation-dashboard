# Customer Segmentation Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes customer demographics, annual income, and spending behavior to identify meaningful customer segments and generate business insights through interactive dashboards.

## Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics
- Study income and spending patterns
- Perform customer segmentation
- Create interactive business dashboards
- Generate actionable insights for decision-making

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Data Visualization
- Data Transformation
- Business Analytics

## Dashboard Features

### Page 1: Customer Overview
- Total Customers KPI
- Average Age KPI
- Average Income KPI
- Average Spending Score KPI
- Customer Distribution by Gender
- Customer Age Distribution
- Annual Income vs Spending Score Analysis

### Page 2: Customer Segmentation Analysis
- Customer Segment Distribution
- Average Spending Score by Segment
- Average Annual Income by Segment

## Customer Segments

The dashboard classifies customers into:

1. High Income - High Spending
2. High Income - Low Spending
3. Low Income - High Spending
4. Low Income - Low Spending

## Key Insights

- Majority of customers belong to the Low Income - Low Spending segment.
- High Income customers show varying spending behaviors.
- Spending patterns are not directly proportional to income levels.
- Customer segmentation helps businesses design targeted marketing strategies.
